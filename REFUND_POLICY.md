# Refund policy — draft

> Paste into Shopify admin → Settings → Policies → Refund policy. Edit wording to match your voice before publishing.

---

We make Olnian in small batches the weeks before we ship. Because of that, a few things are a little different from most supplement brands.

## Orders and payment

We charge your card at checkout. If a batch ships as expected (typically 2–4 weeks after your order), your order is final at that point.

If we can't ship your order for any reason — a testing failure, a batch delay, anything — we'll refund you in full within 3 business days and let you know by email. You don't have to ask.

## First subscription delivery — 30-day guarantee

If this is your first delivery on a new subscription, you have 30 days from the date the order arrives to decide if it's right for you.

To request a refund on your first subscription delivery:

- Email us at hello@olnian.com within 30 days of delivery.
- No need to return the product — keep or compost what's left.
- Refund is issued to your original payment method within 5 business days.

The free gift (gold measuring spoon) is yours to keep either way.

## Subsequent subscription deliveries

After your first delivery, ongoing subscription deliveries are not eligible for the 30-day refund guarantee, but you can:

- **Pause or cancel** at any time from your account page, up to 24 hours before your next charge. No fees.
- **Report a damaged or incorrect shipment** within 14 days for a free replacement or refund.

## Returns

We don't accept returns of opened supplements for safety reasons. If you received the wrong item or a damaged shipment, email us and we'll make it right — no return required.

## One-time purchases

Opened one-time purchases are not refundable. Unopened orders can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund; the customer covers return shipping.

## How to reach us

hello@olnian.com — we reply within one business day.
